How this bot thinks

  • BUY = bias long NatGas (UP).
  • SELL = bias short NatGas (DOWN).
  • NO TRADE = trend unclear or weak.
  • Uses price history (EMAs, RSI, volatility, MACD, NG vs Oil).
  • Uses US + Europe weather now & next 7 days (HDD & CDD).
  • Weather is already included in the confidence number.
